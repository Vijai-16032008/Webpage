My Favorite Books

  • WEATHERING WITH YOU


Author: Makoto Shinkai

Description: Set in a rain-soaked Tokyo, the story centers on Hodaka, a runaway teen, who meets Hina, a girl with the power to control the weather. As they navigate their challenges, they must decide what sacrifices they're willing to make for their future

  • 5 CENTIMETER PER SECOND


Author: Makoto Shinkai

Description: This novel follows Takaki Tōno through three interconnected stories, portraying his relationships and the emotional distance that grows over time and space. It delves into themes of love, loss, and the passage of time.

  • YOUR NAME


Author: Makoto Shinkai

Description: Mitsuha and Taki, two high school students living in different parts of Japan, find themselves inexplicably swapping bodies. As they experience each other's lives, they form a connection that transcends time and space

  • SUZUME


Author: Makoto Shinkai

Description: Seventeen-year-old Suzume encounters a mysterious door in a ruined area of Japan. Upon opening it, she embarks on a journey across the country to close similar doors, preventing disasters and confronting her past.
